Kevin's Top Movie Picks

Below are the top three movies I have seen

#1. Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

This is a coming of age story about an eleven year old boy who finds out he is a wizard. He then goes onto find all sorts of magical treasures and how he is involved.

#2. Here Comes the Boom

This is a underdog story of a teacher who cares for his school so much he becomes a MMA fighter to make enough money to keep the music program alive.

#3 Wall Street: Money Never Sleeps

This is a story about money and greed staring Michael Dougoas who plays an excellent roll.
